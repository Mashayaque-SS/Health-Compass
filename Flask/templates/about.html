About Health Compass

Health Compass is an innovative web application designed to assist individuals in understanding their health status through predictive analytics. Leveraging advanced machine learning models, Health Compass provides insights into two critical health conditions: Heart Disease and Diabetes.

Services:

  • Disease predictions: Users can input their health parameters to receive predictions about the likelihood of heart disease or diabetes.
  • Personal Profile: Users who have registered into Health Compass can store and view their health data whenever they want just by signing into the web app.
  • Tips and Tricks: Users can access specialized Tips/Tricks page dedicated to Heart disease and diabetes for healthy lifestyle remedies.
  • Doctors: Health compass is offering two options for consulting specialized doctors(Cardiologists/Diabetologists).
    • Nearby Doctors: Users can locate nearby Cardiologists and Diabetologists according to their current location.
    • Recommended Doctors: Users can view a list of specialist doctors recommended by health compass. First consultation fee is 20% off, for health compass users.

Key Features:

  • Proper Guidance: Health Compass properly direct their users, whether to consult a doctor or just make user follow a healthy lifestyle accoring to their health condition.
  • Admin Panel: A admin will have all users data who have registered themselves in Health Compass. Analytical data will be helpful for the growth of web app.
  • Consultation offer: A total of 20% off on first consultation for all users of Health Compass.
  • User's Profile: User will have their own personal profiles where they can view and store their health data also can see their registration details.
  • Apprehensible Forms: No subject expert required. Heart Disease and Diabetes input forms are designed in such a way that even a layman can understand(to fill it).
  • Simple UI: Simple and Interactive User Interface which ensures that user will navigate throughout the web app smoothly.
